Bunny is a free (as in freedom) Android media editor, letting you edit images and videos on your phone

It lets you open an existing image or video and modify it.

• Images: apply filters, crop, rotate, change brightness/contrast/saturation
• Videos: crop, change speed, mute audio, stabilize, remove start or end of the video.
• Dark and light themes
• 100% Free and Open Source software. No proprietary dependencies.
